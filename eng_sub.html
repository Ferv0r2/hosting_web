    FAQ

    • Q1.

      Is the number of NFTs minted by the institute limited to 10,000?

    • Keplin Laboratory owns a total of 10,000 Metacells.
      Of these, 500 Metacells are managed by the institute for events and other events
      In other words, total of 9,500 Metacells are minting.
      Metacells will continue to proliferate through proliferation and more Metacells will be issued.

    • Q2.

      How to Evolve?

    • If you have Kepler-452b NFT, you are subject to evolution.
      It evolves into the "Cell - Spout - Adult - Spawnning" order.
      In the case of spawning, when it evolves, it returns to the adult and gets new meta cells randomly.

    • Q3.

      Is it a mining project?

    • This project does not issue or mine independent tokens.
      Issuing tokens itself is not difficult,
      Creating incinerators and forming infrastructure are at a high level.
      However, Kepler-452b is a project equipped with an NFT mining system, not token mining.
